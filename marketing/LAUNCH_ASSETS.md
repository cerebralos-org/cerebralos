## 1. Orynth Submission (English)

**Title:**
CerebraLOS: The Layer of Subconsciousness for AI Agents

**Tagline:**
Your AI remembers everything. That's why it doesn't understand you. Built to forget. Designed to dream.

**Description:**
The world is obsessed with making AI remember everything. Tools like Mem0 build perfect, hierarchical databases of every interaction. They treat AI as a perfect laborer, a machine that never forgets.

But perfect memory is a curse. It leads to context bloat, hallucination, and ultimately, a cold, mechanical interaction. If an AI remembers everything equally, it values nothing.

CerebraLOS is not a database. It is a **Cognitive OS**—a Layer of Subconsciousness for AI agents.
We believe that for AI to truly understand us, it must learn how to forget.

**Key Features:**
1. **Active Forgetting**: Memories decay over time based on entropy. Only what matters survives.
2. **Sleep Job**: While you sleep, your AI dreams. It consolidates fragmented daily logs into profound insights.
3. **Git-Native Architecture**: Your AI's brain is just a private GitHub repository. No vendor lock-in.
4. **Universal Connectors**: Connect any agent (Claude Code, OpenClaw, Manus) like building blocks.
5. **Zero UI**: No dashboards. No complex configurations. It lives in your terminal, breathing quietly in the background.

**Why it matters:**
Current AI agents suffer from "context isolation." They don't share memories. CerebraLOS acts as a shared nervous system. Your local CLI agent and your cloud web agent can now dream together and share the same unified understanding of your goals.

**GitHub Repository:**
https://github.com/cerebralos-org/cerebralos
